# Earth Sciences Syllabus

> The study of the Earth — its structure, processes, history, and environment.

## 7. Earth Sciences

### Geology
- [[courses/geology|Geology]] — Minerals and rocks (igneous, sedimentary, metamorphic), plate tectonics (divergent/convergent/transform boundaries), structural geology (folds, faults, stress/strain), stratigraphy (superposition, correlation), geological time (eons/eras/periods), volcanology, seismology (P/S waves, Richter/moment magnitude), economic geology (ore deposits, petroleum)

### Meteorology & Atmospheric Science
- [[courses/meteorology|Meteorology]] — Atmospheric structure (troposphere→thermosphere), radiation budget ($S = S_0(1-\alpha)/4$ for equilibrium), atmospheric dynamics (Coriolis, geostrophic wind), weather systems (cyclones, fronts, convection), synoptic meteorology (weather maps, forecasting), climate science (Milankovitch cycles, greenhouse effect, climate models: GCMs), severe weather (tornadoes, hurricanes)

### Oceanography
- [[courses/oceanography|Oceanography]] — Physical: ocean circulation (thermohaline, wind-driven, Ekman transport), waves (deep/shallow water, $c = \sqrt{gd}$), tides (lunar/solar forcing). Chemical: salinity, dissolved gases, ocean acidification. Biological: marine ecosystems, primary productivity, coral reefs. Geological: seafloor spreading, sediments, hydrothermal vents

### Paleontology
- [[courses/paleontology|Paleontology]] — Fossil record (preservation, taphonomy), mass extinctions (Big Five, K-Pg boundary), evolutionary paleobiology, biostratigraphy (index fossils), paleoecology, vertebrate/invertebrate paleontology, paleobotany, human evolution (fossil hominins)

### Hydrology
- [[courses/hydrology|Hydrology]] — Water cycle, precipitation (rain gauge, radar, satellite), surface hydrology (runoff, hydrograph, unit hydrograph), groundwater (aquifers, Darcy's law $Q = -KA\frac{dh}{dl}$, well hydraulics), water resources management, floods (return period, frequency analysis), water quality

### Environmental Science
- [[courses/environmental-science|Environmental Science]] — Climate change (greenhouse gases, IPCC, mitigation/adaptation), biodiversity loss, pollution (air, water, soil), sustainability (circular economy, SDGs), environmental policy (Clean Air/Water Acts, Paris Agreement), ecosystem services (valuation), environmental justice
