# Rancho Tres Marias

> Premium Bacanora production honoring family and Mayo heritage. Long-horizon dream, not a hobby.

## Named For

Daniel's mother and two sisters, all named Maria. The name is an act of homage to his family and the Mayo indigenous heritage his maternal line carries.

## Brand Philosophy

"No herede la tradicion. La sembre." (I didn't inherit the tradition. I planted it.)

## Product Lines

| Line | Positioning | Target |
|------|------------|--------|
| Cristal del Desierto | Ultra-premium artisanal | Don Julio / Casa Dragones level |
| Cu'u Libre | Modern, mixology-friendly | Broader market entry |

## Production

- Traditional horno de piedra production
- Located in Denominacion de Origen Bacanora territory, Sonora
- Rooted in the Sonoran regional tradition

## Distribution Strategy

1. Start with Mexico domestic market
2. Expand to US leveraging visa/professional connections built during career

## Timeline

Target launch around age 35 (approximately 2040). This is deliberately long-horizon -- Daniel intends to build the financial foundation and industry knowledge during his tech career first.

## Why This Matters

This project is tied to identity, heritage, family, and a long-term vision of what Daniel's life could mean. The three Marias are real people he loves. The bacanora is an actual regional tradition from his territory. When he talks about it, he is also talking about who he is.
